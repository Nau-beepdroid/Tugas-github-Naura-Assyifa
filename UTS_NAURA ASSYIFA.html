About Mykonos

Mykonos is a contemporary fragrance brand born from the desire to transform everyday moments into something memorable. Established in 2020, the brand combines elegance with accessibility, offering high-quality perfumes that are designed to be both expressive and long-lasting.

Inspired by modern lifestyles and individuality, Mykonos creates scents that are not only worn, but experienced each one capturing a unique mood, story, and identity. Driven by creativity and a deep appreciation for personal expression, Mykonos carefully crafts its collections to suit a wide range of preferences, from fresh and uplifting notes to warm and comforting blends. With a strong presence both online and in selected retail spaces, the brand continues to grow as a trusted choice for fragrance lovers. At its core, Mykonos believes that scent is more than a finishing touch, it is a signature, a memory, and a quiet form of self-expression that lingers long after you leave the room.

Rekomendasi Parfum
  • Dark Secret

    Mykonos Dark Secret is an Indonesian-made extrait de parfum featuring a, mysterious, and, sultry scent profile, combining black cherry, saffron, and praline with warm, woody notes. It is widely described as a long-lasting (12+ hours) unisex fragrance designed for evening or formal wear

  • Affair

    Mykonos Intimate Affair is a gourmand, fruity-floral Extrait de Parfum designed to be romantic and flirty, featuring prominent notes of Raspberry Sorbet, Rose, and Salted Caramel. It is known for its sweet, slightly tart, and creamy profile that is both powdery and woody during the dry-down, offering strong, lasting projection

  • Satin Blanc

    Mykonos Satin Blanc is a floral woody musk extrait de parfum featuring a fresh, crisp, and slightly powdery aroma. Key notes include green apple, bergamot, jasmine tea, and white amber, offering a "snow-kissed" fruity opening that dries down into a warm, creamy, and cozy scent suitable for both men and women

  • Milkdrop

    Mykonos Milk Drops is an Extrait de Parfum featuring a, creamy, and powdery scent profile, focusing on realistic milk, musk, and soft florals. It is designed to be a clean, comforting, and unisex gourmand fragrance with moderate sweetness and notable, lasting soft powdery musky notes.

  • Penthouse

    Mykonos Penthouse is a unisex, Indonesian Extrait de Parfum featuring a fresh, soapy, and clean scent profile designed to smell like a luxurious, high-end hotel shower. It opens with bright citrus and sparkling blackcurrant, settling into a white floral/white tea heart, and dries down to a warm, woody-musky base.
